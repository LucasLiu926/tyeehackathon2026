# 🌀 Aura: The Anti-Burnout Agent

**Manage Energy, Not Just Time.**

Aura is a high-EQ academic companion designed for teens and young adults who are feeling the weight of modern academic pressure. Unlike traditional planners that focus on rigid schedules, Aura focuses on **Energy Architecture**—helping you maintain momentum without hitting "The Wall."

---

## 🚀 The Core Philosophy

Most planners treat humans like machines with infinite batteries. Aura knows better. 

- **Energy > Time:** We don't just schedule tasks; we evaluate their "Brain Battery" drain.
- **Sustainability > Output:** We reward rest blocks as much as work blocks.
- **EQ > IQ:** Aura validates your feelings first, then helps you build a survival plan.

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 🔋 Brain Battery System
A visual representation of your current mental state. The battery drains based on task difficulty and recharges during "Breathe" blocks. Aura adjusts your sprint lengths (15, 20, or 25 mins) based on how much "charge" you have left.

### 🗺️ Dynamic Energy Mapping
Instead of a list, you create an "Energy Map." By scoring the **Drain** (effort) and **Dread** (procrastination factor) of a task, Aura identifies "Doom Clusters"—spots where you're likely to burn out—and automatically redistributes them.

### 🏃 Sprint + Breathe Schedule
Aura builds a personalized "Gameplan" that alternates high-focus sprints with science-backed reset micro-actions (e.g., box breathing, no-screen walks, hydration).

### 📓 Mood Journal (Vibe Log)
Track your energy patterns over time. Aura uses this data to provide insights like *"You always crash at 7 PM on Wednesdays"* to help you plan your week more effectively.

### 💬 Aura Chat
An empathetic AI interface that helps you break through procrastination spirals or "Zombie Mode" with proactive, supportive coaching.
